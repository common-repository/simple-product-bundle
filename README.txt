With Simple Product Bundle you can create offers and capture the attention of your customers. Create product offers.


== Description ==
Simple Product Bundle allows you to create offers on a specific product. In addition to the specific offer of the single product, you can create an offer consisting of several products. This offer may include the product the user is viewing or may contain other products. Thanks to our plugin you can create offers on the specific product or recommend a promotion with different products. (Usually purchased together, you might be interested in this offer)
With Simple Product Bundle you can add a percentage discount on the total offer you have created. In this way, in addition to recommending an offer of products with a discount, you increase interest in the offer.


Features free version of the plugin:


1) Create a single offer for each product
2) Choose whether to activate a discount on the offer
3) Choose the title of the bundle
4) Choose the order of the bundle products
5) If a bundle product is automatically out of stock, the bundle will be deactivated with an alert in the product sheet


By purchasing the PRO version of the Simple WooCommerce Product Bundle plugin you will be able to use various functionalities that are not included in the free version.

== Installation ==
1) Download the plugin from the WordPress directory or download it here and upload into  / wp-content / plugins / folder
2) Install the plugin on your site and activate it
3) Access the backend of your product, in the Product section you will see a new menu  \"Create Offer\" and activate your bundle.
